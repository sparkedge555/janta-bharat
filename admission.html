What to Bring

Required Documents

Please bring original documents along with one set of photocopies at the time of admission.

Birth Certificate

Original birth certificate issued by municipality / hospital / gram panchayat. Mandatory for all classes.

Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar card of the child and parent / guardian. Both originals and photocopies required.

Transfer Certificate (TC)

Previous school's Transfer Certificate. Required for Class 1 and above. Not needed for Nursery / LKG / UKG.

Passport-Size Photographs

4 recent colour passport-size photographs of the child on white background. Must be taken within 3 months.

Address Proof

Any one of: Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Ration Card, or Electricity Bill. Must show current residential address.

Previous Report Card

Last year's progress report / mark sheet (if available). Helpful for Class 2 and above. Not strictly mandatory.

Note: All original documents will be returned after verification. Please bring 1 photocopy set of each document. If a document is unavailable, contact us at 8877225674 and we will guide you on alternatives.
