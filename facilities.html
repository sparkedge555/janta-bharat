Residential Life

Life Inside Our Hostel

Since we are an "Aawasiya" (residential) Vidyalaya, hostel life is at the heart of what we do. Here's a closer look at daily routine, meals, care, and safety.

🛏️ Dormitory
🍽️ Mess Hall
📖 Study Hall

Our hostel students follow a structured daily routine that balances academics, nutrition, recreation, and rest:

  • 5:30 AM — Wake up & freshen up
  • 6:00 AM — Morning exercise / yoga
  • 7:00 AM — Morning self-study
  • 7:45 AM — Breakfast
  • 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM — School & classes
  • 4:00 PM — Sports & play time
  • 6:00 PM — Evening study (supervised)
  • 8:00 PM — Dinner & lights out

See the full hour-by-hour schedule on our Academics page.

Meals are balanced, hygienically prepared, and served three times a day:

  • Breakfast: Milk, bread/paratha, seasonal options on rotation
  • Lunch: Rice/roti, dal, seasonal vegetable, occasional curd
  • Dinner: Roti/rice, dal, sabzi, with a light evening snack beforehand

The full weekly mess menu is displayed on the hostel notice board and can be shared on request — please contact the school office for the latest menu.

Each hostel block is supervised by dedicated warden and caretaker staff who live on or near campus and remain available around the clock. They oversee daily routines, attend to student needs, coordinate with teachers on academic progress, and are the first point of contact for any concern a child may have.

For warden contact details or to discuss your child's hostel stay, please reach the school office at 8877225674.

The safety of every resident student is our top priority:

  • Boundary-walled, CCTV-monitored campus and hostel blocks
  • 24×7 warden & caretaker presence in every hostel block
  • First-aid room on campus with prompt parent notification for medical needs
  • Separate, age-appropriate hostel arrangements
  • Regular communication with parents regarding student wellbeing
