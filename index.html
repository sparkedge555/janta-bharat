Welcome to Our School

A second home for every student

Since 2015, Janta Bharat Aawasiya Vidyalaya — established under the Janta Bharat Educational And Public Welfare Trust — has provided a warm, disciplined residential environment in Kosiyapatti, Supaul district, Bihar, where children from Nursery to Class 8 receive focused academic attention alongside the comfort of a true home away from home.

Under the visionary leadership of Chairman Shri Pradeep Kumar, our teachers, hostel staff, and management work together every day to keep students safe, well-fed, and inspired — because education works best when a child feels secure.
