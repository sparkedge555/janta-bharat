Welcome to Our School

A second home for every student

Since 2015, Janta Bharat Aawasiya Vidyalaya has provided a warm, disciplined residential environment in Kosiyapatti where children from Nursery to Class 8 receive focused academic attention alongside the comfort and care of a true home away from home.

Our teachers, hostel staff, and management work together every day to keep students safe, well-fed, and motivated to learn — because education works best when a child feels secure.

Why Families Choose Us

Everything your child needs to thrive

From a secure hostel to qualified teachers and a green, safe campus — here is what sets our school apart.

Residential Hostel

Clean, supervised dormitories with nutritious meals and a caring house-staff team around the clock.

Qualified Teachers

Experienced, dedicated educators who know every child by name and nurture steady academic progress.

Safe, Secure Campus

A boundary-walled, supervised campus with CCTV coverage so children can play and learn freely.

Modern Library

A growing collection of story books, reference material, and learning resources for every age group.

School Transport

Safe pick-up and drop facility connecting nearby villages and towns to our campus.

Sports & Activities

Daily playtime, annual sports day, and cultural events that build confidence beyond the classroom.
