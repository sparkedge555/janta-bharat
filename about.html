Our Journey

A Story of Purpose, Service & Education

Janta Bharat Aawasiya Vidyalaya was established in 2015 under the Janta Bharat Educational And Public Welfare Trust, registered under the Government of Bihar. Situated in the heart of Koriapatti East, Tribeniganj Block, Kosiyapatti, Supaul, our school was born from a singular belief: that every child — regardless of background or geography — deserves access to quality education in a nurturing, safe environment.

Under the visionary leadership of Chairman Shri Pradeep Kumar, we have grown from a small community school into a trusted residential institution serving families across Supaul district and neighbouring regions. Our dedicated team of 28+ teachers and support staff work together every day to ensure that each of our 450+ students receives personal attention, structured learning, and the warmth of a second home.

Today, with a 95% annual pass rate, government recognition (UDISE: 10061102019, Reg. No: 208111120211120145050), and a growing reputation for excellence, we continue to build on our founding mission — one student at a time. Join us for the 2025–26 session.

A Message from Our Chairman

Shri Pradeep Kumar

Chairman & Founder

"When we founded Janta Bharat Aawasiya Vidyalaya in 2015, our vision was simple yet profound — to create a place where children from all backgrounds could receive the quality education they rightfully deserve. Education is not merely about textbooks and examinations; it is about building character, instilling values, and preparing young minds to face the world with confidence and compassion.

Over the past decade, I have had the privilege of witnessing hundreds of children grow from shy, uncertain young learners into confident, curious students — and that transformation is what motivates every member of our school family every single day. Our residential facility is not just a hostel; it is a second home where children feel safe, loved, and inspired to achieve their very best.

To every parent who entrusts us with their child's future — I give you my personal assurance that we will never take that responsibility lightly. Your child's safety, happiness, and growth are at the very centre of everything we do at Janta Bharat Aawasiya Vidyalaya. Together, we are building the Bihar of tomorrow."

Shri Pradeep Kumar — Chairman, Janta Bharat Educational & Public Welfare Trust
Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Every decision we make — from curriculum to hostel management — is guided by these three pillars.

Our Mission

To provide every child with access to quality, holistic education in a safe residential environment — equipping them with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible, contributing members of society.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted residential school in Bihar — where students from all backgrounds thrive academically, develop strong character, and carry forward the values of discipline, compassion, and excellence.

Core Values

Integrity in teaching, compassion in care, discipline in daily life, and inclusivity in practice. We celebrate diversity, encourage curiosity, and treat every student as an individual with unique potential.
