How We Teach

Our Teaching Approach & Methodology

At Janta Bharat Aawasiya Vidyalaya, we believe education works best when children feel safe, curious, and engaged. Our bilingual teaching methodology — combining Hindi and English — ensures no child is left behind due to a language barrier. Experienced teachers use activity-based learning, storytelling, and regular revision to make concepts stick. With small classroom batches and a residential setting, our teachers know every student personally.

Activity-Based Learning

Concepts taught through hands-on activities, experiments, and demonstrations for better retention.

Personalised Attention

Small class sizes ensure every student receives focused attention from dedicated teachers.

Regular Assessments

Weekly tests, monthly evaluations, and term exams to track progress and address gaps early.

Structured Study Hours

Daily supervised study periods in the hostel ensure homework completion and exam readiness.

Bilingual Instruction

Teaching in both Hindi and English to make learning accessible and build strong communication skills.
