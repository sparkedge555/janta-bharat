Quick Answers

Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Call us at 8877225674 and we'll be happy to help.

📅 When do admissions open?

Admissions for the new session (2025–26) are currently open. We enroll students from Nursery to Class 8. Seats are limited, so we recommend enquiring early to avoid disappointment.

🏠 Is hostel facility available?

Yes! We offer a full residential hostel for both boys and girls with clean dormitories, nutritious meals (3 times daily), and 24×7 supervised care by experienced hostel staff.

📚 What classes are offered?

We offer classes from Nursery, LKG, UKG through Class 1 to Class 8, in bilingual medium (Hindi & English). Both residential (hostel) and day scholar options are available.

💰 How much is the fee?

Our fees are transparent and affordable. Please visit our Fee Structure page for complete details, or call us for a personalised discussion based on class and hostel requirements.

📋 What documents are needed?

For admission you'll need: Birth Certificate, Aadhaar Card (child & parent), Transfer Certificate (Class 1+), 4 passport-size photos, and Address Proof. See our Admission page for full details.

⏰ What are your office hours?

Our school office is open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. For urgent hostel matters, our hostel staff are available 24×7. WhatsApp enquiries are answered within a few hours even on Sundays.
